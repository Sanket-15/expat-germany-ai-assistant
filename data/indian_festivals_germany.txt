At Wedesi , our mission is to enhance the experiences of third places. We are a foundation that celebrates diversity and culture.

What we do at Wedesi
Our events and activations bring people of all walks of life together to celebrate culture and food. We create shared spaces where differences are celebrated.

Community Events
We organize memorable events where people can network, get inspired, and get in touch with their fellow expats and locals.

Network + Market
Thanks to our strong and extensive knowledge of the German market , we can be the guide and reference point for any business wishing to enter european market. Connect with startups from diverse sectors and regions, fostering a cross-cultural exchange of ideas and innovation.

Indian Gastronomy 🇮🇳
We promote the Indian gastronomy in Germany. Authentic Indian food has finally arrived in Europe. Starting with Berlin’s growing south Asian population Wedesi brings you the best of Germany's Indian chefs.

Make it in Germany 🇩🇪
Wedesi provides Integration help and digital support to help expats and students find their way in germany with our partners.

Wedesi Events in Berlin

Diwali In Berlin
Experience Love, Light and Laughter . Join us every year for Diwali, a festival of lights in the heart of Berlin. Experience the grand Diwali Spectacle the Indian style. Our 4000+ attendance, 15+ artist performances and 20+ Authentic Indian Food stalls.

Holi in Berlin
Holi, the Indian festival of colors, celebrates spring with vibrant Gulal powder, uniting everyone in harmony and joy. Join Wedesi for Holi In Berlin - a day of organic colors, incredible Bollywood music, 15+ food stalls, a Desi Flea Market, and unforgettable memories.

The Great Indian Food festival
Experience the rich flavors and cultural diversity of India at The Great Indian Food Festival in Berlin indulge in aromatic and delicious Indian food and drinks, prepared by Berlin's biggest Indian chefs and Indian pop-up concepts from Germany.
